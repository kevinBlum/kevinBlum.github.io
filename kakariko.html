Kakariko Village is one of the two main settlements of Hyrule. Founded by the people known as the Sheikah, this village has many ties to the Royal Family. The village is located in the northeastern region of Hyrule, between the castle and Death Mountain. The windmill, a key feature of the town, is used to draw up water into the central well. Other important locations in this town include the graveyard, which is the final resting place to many of Hyrule's great leaders. There are many small shops in this village, including the famous potion shop and the shooting gallery.
