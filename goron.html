Goron City, as the name implies, is the home of the Gorons and their chief Darunia. This great city in stone is located halfway up the Death Mountain trail, and the main entrance into the mountain's scorching crater. The rocky terrain makes it difficult to traverse the whole area unless one is properly equipped with bombs to clear the way. Hikers must be wary of the Dodongos that inhabit the area, which can be taken care of using smoke. Inside the city, cave paintings of the Goron's history can be seen along all the walls on each floor.
