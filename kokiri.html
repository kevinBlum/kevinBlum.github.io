The Kokiri Forest is a village located in the eastern section of Hyrule, with Hyrule Field to the west, the Lost Woods to the east, and Zora's Domain to the north. This forest is inhabited by a race known as Kokiri, and their guardian spirit the Great Deku Tree. The forest is also known to be the source of all life, keeping the order in the world and preventing outsiders from entering the forbidden forest. The Kokiri Forest is the hometown of the Hylian hero Link.
